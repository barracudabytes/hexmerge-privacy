Contact Support

Email: barracudabytesdev@gmail.com

When You Contact Us

To help us resolve your issue faster, include:

  • your device model
  • your iOS version
  • your app version
  • a short description of the issue
  • steps to reproduce it
  • screenshots if helpful

Common Help Topics

I watched a rewarded ad but did not get the reward

Close and reopen the game, then try again. If the issue continues, contact support and include the approximate time the ad was shown and what reward you expected.

My progress or coins look wrong

Please include:

  • what happened just before the issue
  • whether you reopened the app
  • whether you were using the store, continue, or undo features

I purchased No Ads or a coin pack and it did not appear

Please contact support with:

  • the product you purchased
  • the approximate purchase time
  • a screenshot of your App Store receipt or purchase history if available

How ads work in HexMerge

HexMerge is intended to use optional rewarded ads for gameplay-related bonuses, such as:

  • continuing after a loss
  • receiving certain additional power-up uses

Response Time

We aim to respond as quickly as practical, but response times may vary.
