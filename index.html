HexMerge Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Barracuda Bytes ("we," "our," or "us") operates the HexMerge mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains what information HexMerge may collect, how that information is used, when it may be shared, and what choices you have. This policy is intended to support release on the Apple App Store and use of Google AdMob rewarded advertising.

Summary: HexMerge is a puzzle game. You can play without creating an account. The app stores gameplay-related settings and progress locally on your device. The app may display rewarded ads and may offer in-app purchases. We do not require your name, email address, or account registration to use the app.

Information We Collect

Information stored locally on your device

HexMerge stores certain gameplay and app settings locally on your device, including:

  • high score
  • current score and gameplay state needed for resume or continue features
  • tutorial completion state
  • coin balance and in-game economy state
  • sound and haptics preferences
  • no-ads purchase status

This information is used to make the app function properly and improve the player experience.

Advertising data

If you choose to watch a rewarded ad, Google AdMob and its partners may collect or receive information such as:

  • device identifiers
  • IP address
  • coarse location inferred from IP address
  • advertising and diagnostics information
  • ad performance and fraud-prevention data

This data may be used to provide, measure, and protect advertising services. Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your consent, platform permissions, region, and device settings.

Purchase-related data

If you make an in-app purchase, payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment card information.

Support communications

If you contact us for support, we may receive the information you send us, such as your email address and the details of your request.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • operate and maintain the game
  • save progress and settings
  • provide rewarded advertising
  • restore purchases and no-ads status where applicable
  • respond to support requests
  • detect abuse, fraud, or technical issues

Advertising and Third-Party Services

HexMerge uses Google AdMob to provide rewarded ads. Rewarded ads are optional and may be offered in situations such as continuing after a loss or receiving certain additional in-game power-up uses. We do not intend to show banner ads during active gameplay.

The app uses third-party services, including Google AdMob and Apple, which may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

For more information about how Google uses data, see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services

Tracking and Permissions

If required by Apple or applicable law, the app may request permission for tracking through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before using certain advertising features that rely on tracking.

If you do not grant permission, advertising may still be shown in a non-personalized form where supported.

Data Sharing

We may share information with service providers that help operate the app, including:

  • Apple, for App Store distribution and in-app purchases
  • Google AdMob, for rewarded advertising

We do not sell personal information for money in the ordinary sense. However, some advertising-related data transfers may be considered "sharing" under certain privacy laws.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you may have the right to request access to, deletion of, or correction of personal information, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information as those terms are defined under California law.

While we do not sell personal information for money, certain advertising activities may be considered "sharing" under California law. You can limit this by adjusting your device settings, including App Tracking Transparency permissions where available.

Legal Basis for Processing (EEA/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we process information under one or more of the following legal bases:

  • performance of a contract, such as providing app functionality you request
  • legitimate interests, such as maintaining app security, functionality, and fraud prevention
  • consent, where required, such as for certain personalized advertising activities

Data Retention

Gameplay settings and progress are generally stored on your device unless a platform provider requires related transactional records for purchases or advertising operations.

Support messages and related correspondence may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request and maintain support records.

Data Deletion

Because most gameplay-related data is stored locally on your device, you can generally delete that data by uninstalling the app. Transactional records related to purchases or advertising may be retained by Apple, Google, or other service providers under their own policies.

Children's Privacy

HexMerge is a general-audience puzzle game and is not directed to children under 13, or under the age at which parental consent is required by applicable law in a user's region. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information inappropriately, contact us and we will review the request.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding access, deletion, correction, restriction, or objection to certain processing of personal information. To make a privacy-related request, contact us using the support information below.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information associated with the app, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own by Apple, Google, or other service providers involved in app delivery, purchase processing, or advertising.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
